When you use Azure, [reliability is a shared responsibility](/azure/reliability/concept-shared-responsibility). Microsoft provides a range of capabilities to support resiliency and recovery. You're responsible for understanding how those capabilities work within all the services that you use and for selecting the capabilities that you need to meet your business objectives and uptime goals.
